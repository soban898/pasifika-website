Pasifika Day 2026

Join Us for a Day of Culture & Community

Pasifika Day 2026 is a vibrant community celebration bringing together the Polynesian and Indigenous Aboriginal communities for a day of culture, connection, and unity. This event aims to support youth, celebrate heritage, and create meaningful opportunities for everyone involved.

  • Date: 8 August 2026
  • Time: 10:00 AM – 6:00 PM
  • Location: Blacktown Showground

What to Expect

  • 🎟️ Live charity auction supporting PCYC Blacktown
  • 🎭 Cultural performances and entertainment
  • 🍔 A wide range of food trucks
  • 🎡 Family-friendly rides and activities
  • 🧺 Picnic and seating areas
  • 🏪 Community stalls and local vendors
  • 📢 Information about our programs
  • 🤝 Opportunities to join and get involved

More than just an event, Pasifika Day is about bringing people together, strengthening community bonds, and creating a positive future for our youth.

About Us

Empowering Pasifika Communities Through Culture & Connection

Pasifika is a community-driven organisation dedicated to celebrating culture, empowering youth, and strengthening connections across communities. We create opportunities for individuals to grow through cultural programs, mentorship, and community initiatives.

  • Preserving Culture & Identity

    We celebrate Pasifika traditions, language, and heritage through meaningful cultural engagement and events.

  • Empowering Youth & Future Leaders

    Supporting young people through structured programs, mentorship, and pathways that build confidence and opportunity.

Our Ambassadors

Meet Our Community Ambassadors

Our ambassadors inspire and guide youth through sport, culture, and leadership.

Linn Sandström

Boxing Ambassador

Linn is a globally ranked professional boxer inspiring youth through resilience, discipline, and empowerment in sport. She has competed internationally and continues to advocate for women in sport, encouraging young athletes to chase their goals.

Dharringarra (Dboy)

Cultural Ambassador

A proud Indigenous boxer representing culture, strength, and community while inspiring the next generation. He carries his story, identity, and values into every fight, motivating youth to stay connected to their roots and rise with purpose.
