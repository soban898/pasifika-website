LLF Youth Boxing Development Program

A Legends Life Foundation (LLF) Initiative

The LLF Youth Boxing Development Program is a 10-week initiative created to support and empower Pasifika youth, particularly those facing disadvantage. Through boxing, mentorship, and structured guidance, the program helps young people build confidence, discipline, and a strong sense of purpose.

Program Overview

Delivered in partnership with local gyms and community leaders, this program combines physical training with personal development. Participants take part in boxing sessions, leadership activities, and mentorship opportunities that encourage growth both inside and outside the gym.

Why This Program Matters

Many young people have the passion and potential to succeed in sport but lack access to resources and support. This program helps bridge that gap by providing guidance, structure, and opportunities for youth to grow into confident and capable individuals.

Program Timeline (10 Weeks)

  • Weeks 1–2: Introduction to boxing fundamentals
  • Weeks 3–4: Skill development and goal setting
  • Week 5: Mid-program assessment
  • Weeks 6–7: Advanced training and nutrition education
  • Weeks 8–9: Team building and preparation
  • Week 10: Final showcase and celebration

Program Components

  • Boxing Training Sessions
  • Nutritional Workshops
  • Life Skills & Educational Sessions
  • Guest Speakers & Mentorship
  • Team Building Activities
  • Final Exhibition Event

Expected Outcomes

  • Improved physical fitness and boxing skills
  • Increased confidence and resilience
  • Better decision-making and behaviour
  • Stronger sense of community and belonging
  • Development of positive habits and routines

Program Info

  • Duration: 10 Weeks
  • Sessions: 3 per week
  • Target: Pasifika Youth
  • Focus: Fitness, Discipline, Growth
